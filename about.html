The Basic Gospel is a ministry dedicated to the practical teaching of the Christian life from the Scriptures. Posts are geared to new and young believers seeking to grow in their faith in Christ.

My name is Gabriel Guerrero and I have been a Christian since 2003. Since then, I have learned a lot in my growth as a Christian and have always had a passion to teach. I started this website as a companion to the New Testament Outreach Bibles that I hand out. I want basic bible teaching on the New Testament to be available for free to everyone.

The Bible is a deep book, but I believe anyone who gives their life to Jesus Christ can grown in wisdom, knowledge, and understanding with daily reading, prayer, and a little guidance. I appreciate you stopping by and hope the content was a blessing to you.
